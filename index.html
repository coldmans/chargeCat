How it works

Three things happen.
All of them delightful.

🔌

Plug in

Connect your charger like you always do. Charge Cat detects the power event instantly.

🚪

Door opens

A tiny door appears in the corner of your screen. It swings open with a soft creak.

🐱

Cat says hi

A little cat peeks out, bows, smiles, then goes back inside. 2 seconds of pure joy.

Details

Small app. Big feelings.

🖥

Screen corner overlay

Shows up in the bottom-left or bottom-right. Doesn't block your work. Disappears on its own.

🔋

Battery-aware expressions

Low battery? The cat looks tired. Full charge? Party mode. The cat reacts to your Mac's state.

🎵

Menu bar resident

Lives quietly in your menu bar. No dock icon clutter. Toggle position and auto-detect from there.

🍪

Zero overhead

Native Swift + AppKit. No Electron. No web views. Uses virtually no CPU or memory when idle.
